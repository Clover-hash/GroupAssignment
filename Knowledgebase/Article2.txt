I LOVE HARU URARA 

Uma Musume: Pretty Derby is a multimedia franchise by Cygames that reimagines real Japanese racehorses as “horse girls” who train, race, and perform as idols. At its core, the franchise blends competitive sports storytelling with slice-of-life character development, creating a unique hybrid that appeals to both racing fans and anime lovers.

Each character in Uma Musume is inspired by a real racehorse, inheriting their name, personality traits, and historical achievements. This connection adds emotional depth, as players often learn about real racing history through the game and anime. After races, the winners perform live concerts, symbolizing victory, effort, and celebration rather than traditional sports trophies.

The franchise’s success lies in its ability to make a niche topic—horse racing—approachable, emotional, and entertaining for a younger audience. By combining strategy, narrative, and music, Uma Musume has carved out a distinct identity in the anime and gaming industry.
