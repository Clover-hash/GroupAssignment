My Food Order Is Missing Items or Is Incorrect

Answer:
We’re sorry about the inconvenience. If your order is missing items or does not match what you ordered, please report it through the app as soon as possible.

Open Help > Food Orders, select the order, and choose “Missing or incorrect items.” Depending on the issue, you may receive a refund, partial refund, or voucher.

Providing clear details helps us resolve the issue faster and improve service quality.
